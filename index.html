Mission

A Mission statement is one of the most powerful tools you have for leading your business or organization to success. A mission statement takes the 'why' of what you do and consolidates it into about one to two sentences. A strong mission statement will contain four key elements: value, inspiration, plausibility, specificity.

Vission

A Vission statement describes what a company desires to achieve in the long-run, generally in a time frame of five to ten years, or sometimes even longer. The main elements of an effective vision statement are forward looking, motivating and inspirational, reflective of a company's culture and core values etc.

Goals

A Goal is an idea of the future or desired result that a person or a group of people envision, plan and commit to achieve. people endeavour to reach goals within a finite time by setting deadlines. A Goal is roughly similar to a purpose or aimn the anticipated result which guides reaction, or an end, which is an object, either a physical object or an abstract object, that has intrinsic value.
